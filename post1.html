You need to join the data science conversation

 

Posted by Kylie Reiri on 13 May 2017

No technical background? Never seen code in your life? Heard people talking about data science and you’re wondering what it’s all about? Perfect. I’d like to extend my formal invitation for you to join the data science conversation.

What is data science anyway?

Data science is simply the science of learning from data to make more informed decisions. It requires a willingness and commitment to working in a new way.

From my experience in a data science team, the hardest part of doing data science is not number crunching or writing code. It is the behavioural change; convincing people to try something new.

We all need to work in a new way to harness the benefits of the world’s most valuable resource.

Technical experts, managers, decision makers, teachers; data science needs all of us. Which is why I am writing this blog.

The more people who understand the benefits of data science, and what is involved in doing it, the further we will advance.

I promise to keep this blog free of equations, free of code, and free of unnecessary complexity. I will aim to explain all aspects of data science in the simplest way I can.

You can join the conversation no matter your background. We need you too.
