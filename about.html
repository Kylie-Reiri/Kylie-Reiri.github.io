About this blog

Not an analyst? Never seen code in your life? Heard a few analytics buzz words and you’re wondering what it’s all about? Perfect. I’d like to extend my formal invitation for you to sit at the analytics table.

There are many blogs about analytics. This one is different. It is not technical. Yes I am an analyst, but my blogs aren’t for other analysts. They are for anyone else who to wants understand what analysts do and how to work with us.

Analysts you're welcome to tag along too, but you’ve had a seat all along so you have to sit in the back.

Why “a seat at the analytics table"

The more people who understand analytics the better. I want to extend the conversation beyond analysts. You can sit at the table with us without technical knowledge, we need you. We move faster alone but further together.

About me

I am proud to be Māori, I am fast approaching 30, I live in Wellington and my Hogwarts house is Hufflepuff.

I have been using data for 10 years. My passion is simple; using data and evidence to make more informed decisions. My training in Statistics helps me do this, but I am far from traditional. Simple or complex I’ll use the right method for the job. The most important thing to me is what we decide to do differently as a result of knowing something new.

Outside of working with data I love to cook, read fantasy, paint, do yoga, scuba dive (preferably somewhere warmer than Wellington) and spend time with my whānau.
